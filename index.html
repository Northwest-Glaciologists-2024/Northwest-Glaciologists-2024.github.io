The Northwest Glaciologists annual meeting is an informal gathering with no abstracts, no formal registration, and a strong history of student participation. A small registration fee (no more than $50) will be collected at the door. Northwest Glaciologists is a branch meeting of IGS and follows the IGS Code of Conduct.
